Profile

Prof. Nayana K is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at GM University, Davanagere. She joined GMU on January 2, 2023, with nearly 16 years of experience in academics, research, and administration, gained at PESITM, Shivamogga, and SCT Institute of Technology, Bengaluru.

At GMU, she also serves as the Program Director for M.Tech in Deep Learning. She is responsible for overseeing and coordinating all academic activities of the program, including curriculum planning, course delivery, student mentoring, research facilitation, and ensuring overall academic excellence. Additionally, she is the Reporter from School of Computer Science and Technology (SCST) for GMU Times, the university's quarterly campus magazine, which highlights academic, cultural, and research activities.

Prof. Nayana K is also actively engaged in critical academic areas such as Academic Co-coordinator, Research coordinator for Centre of Excellence AIML, Technical Seminar coordinator, BOS member for PG programs.

Educational Background

  • Ph.D. (Pursuing): Computer Science and Engineering from Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belagavi. Research domain: Deep Learning with thesis title "ECG Anomaly Detection using LSTM Autoencoder and SHAP for Explainability" at PES College of Engineering, Mandya.
  • M.Tech: Information and Communication Technology (ICT) in Computer Science and Engineering from VTU, Belagavi in 2011 at SJCE Mysore.
  • B.E.: Electronics and Communication Engineering from VTU in 2007 at GSSSITW, Mysore.
